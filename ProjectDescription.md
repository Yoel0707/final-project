## The Theme

My game is a fantansial adventure where the player aims to defeat an evil socerer. 

## Planned classes

Player- Stores level, inventory, energy 
Items- Shows what you have picked up so far and how much more space you have left
Character- Npcs that may give you items or try to battle you 
Character Bundle- Tracks the 5 things that you need to do in order to fight the final villan
Location- Gives where the player is located currently


## Planned Limtied Resource 

Energy will need to be managed, which will be used for travelling and fighting. You can also restore energy. 

## Plan for Handling Time

The way time works is that you will have a set number of days in order to prepare yourself and get ready for the boss. If you complete all the five requirements by the time finshing, you lose. 

## EC 

I plan to implement the shortest path recommendation system for extra credit. 

## Tradeoff System 

A Dark Magic Shop can act as the tradeoff system, where rare things may be bought here but may also bring a risk such as temporary increased energy consumption, time going faster, etc. 

## Mapping Style 

The map will be a text based connected map so each place will be connected with each other

## Win/Lose Condition

The player will lose if they don't complete all of the requirements before time finishes.
