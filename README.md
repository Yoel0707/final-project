# CSCI 1300 Final Project

## Theme

Fantasy 

## Goal

Gain enough levels to become strong, contiously train to uncover new powers, and find new items to be able to beat the evil soceror that threatens the existence of humanity. 

## How to compile and run

There will be a file called "game" and you run the code using the basic three Wall, Werror, and Wpedantic.

## How to play

You start off as the weakest wizard in your class, not being able to do very much. However, when the evil socerer called Lord Farquad kills the entire attacking santion on the wizard army, the king has no chose but to put you through a quest to save humanity. You must first venture the wild and gain the three most important bases of power: Fire, Water, and Earth. You will have to fight monsters you encounter made by the evil socerer either by using melee items, the elemental power you learn, or some other special items you pick up. You also need to pick up two artifacts to understand the weaknesses of the socerer and how toe nchance hyour strength. Only by getting these five things will you be able to fight the king. You will aso interact with characters that may serve a purpose in your quest. 

## Classes

Player- Stores level, inventory, energy 
Items- Shows what you have picked up so far and how much more space you have left
Character- Npcs that may give you items or try to battle you 
Character Bundle- Tracks the 5 things that you need to do in order to fight the final villan
Location- Gives where the player is located currently
